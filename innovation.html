Biological Signal Analysis Instruments

INVENIUM provides customized systems for measurement and analysis of the EMG, ECG and EEG signals. Our engineers have mastered the technology for measurement of the biological signals which are in micro-volt level and are often chaotic and contaminated with EMI noise. We are able to provide both tethered and wireless systems with large bandwidth.

 Designed to resolve the challenges of subject mobility, ultra-portable BLUEMYO offers unique capabilities. The exceptional lightweight design is an ideal choice for all ages of patients and subjects. BLUEMYO wireless EMG system enables the subject to have total freedom of movement and facilitates easy and fast data collection, while providing high quality measurements. Advance technical design, and exceptional features of the BLUEMYO will enable you to conduct EMG measurement in any environment and situation.Ideal for experienced or novice users alike, BLUEMYO is designed to make EMG recordings effortless. BLUEMYO has a budget constrained design with zero continually operating budget. 

BLUEMYO is able to measure up to eight EMG channels. The EMG is measured via surface electrodes and data transfer to the PC is accomplished via Bluetooth®. Pre/post filtering removes the possibility of sample aliasing and EMG artifact under all operating conditions.  Each input channel can be used to read other sensory information, e.g.: EEG, force gauges, accelerometers, foot switches and respiration sensors.

Small and low cost MYOSENS facilitates easy and fast EMG data collection, while providing high quality measurement. Ideal for experienced or novice users alike, MYOSENS is designed to make EMG recordings effortless. MYOSENS has a budget constrained design with zero continually operating budget required. 

MYOSENS is able to measure up to four EMG channels. The EMG is measured via surface electrodes and data is transferred to PC via USB. Pre/post filtering removes the possibility of sample aliasing and EMG artifacts under all operating conditions. The device is powered by USB cable eliminates need for bulky power module. Medical device safety requirements are satisfied with full electrical isolation of USB signal and power lines. Each input channel can be used to read other sensory information, such as EEG, force gauges, accelerometers, foot switches and respiration sensors.

The Active EMG Electrode is an advanced solution for problem of interference pickup by electrode cables, providing the best possible suppression of interference by impedance transformation and signal amplification on electrode. The AE-100 electrode is designed with a constant gain of 300 and a built-in pass-band filter with 25-500 Hz bandwidth. The electrodes are designed using two cone-shape silver contact. The plastic sensor enclosure provides electric isolation between skin and electrode electronics. The electrode is designed to be interfaced with the MYOSENS and BLUEMYO EMG systems, however it can be used with any DAQ system following proper signaling.


MYOGAME is a platform developed at INVENIUM to play games using surface electromyography signals. The platform is using MYOSENS and BLUEMYO EMG measurement platfrom and could be used in conjunction of XNA game development platform to build EMG-based games.
